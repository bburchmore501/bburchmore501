Topic 4 - Online Webinars

The Online Live Class

The main direct interaction between the mentor and the students is the live webinar. These forums are where the mentor can accomplish unique outcomes, and where the students can explore specific objectives for their own learning journey.

The webinar is an interaction first, and a delivery of content second. In this context the mentor must use the webinar to achieve specific goals in order for it to be effective.

Intended Learning

Participants in this topic will examine the detailed skills behind conducting an effective webinar. Various techniques of presentation and content delivery will be explored and participants will explore the user experience of online media.

Threshold Concepts

When considering the most important discoverings of any topic, the threshold concepts are those which will irreversibly change the way you think about the subject from here on. The following are considered threshold concepts that, once understood, will forever change your understanding.

  • Students will engage more with open opinion than with facts and knowledge.
  • Students can be encouraged and motivated by each other more than by the mentor.

Questions to Ponder

Consider the following questions as a guide to understanding this topic. Ask yourself how well you understand these principles and how satisfied you are that you can explain them to others.

  • What is the easiest and fastest way to get students to contribute during a webinar?
  • What is the benefit of having students see each otherâ€™s comments?
  • What are the dangers of allowing students to verbally participate?

Considerations and Observations

As we explore communication theory take these considerations into account when forming your own opinions.

  • Students may often be doing other things (or tempted to) during your webinar.
  • The webinar may discuss or expand on other content, but should not simply repeat it.
  • A webinar must include encouragement and opportunities for students to actively participate.

Activity Task

To practice this topic and to embed its knowledge students should conduct the following task. Do this in your own time when the opportunity arises.

Review the recording of an online learning webinar. Consider the way in which it was conducted and how well it benefited from the content in this topic.

Define which areas of the webinar delivery could be improved to get better results, or to better engage students. Consider how you can incorporate those improvements into your next webinar.
