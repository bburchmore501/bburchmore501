Online Mentoring - Short Course

Short Course for Online Teachers

This is a 4-topic short course aimed at profossional teachers and mentors who deliver online education to adult students.

Participants can access the content, download the files, and complete regular tasks for each topic. Master the subtle art of online teaching.

Topic 1 - Fundamentals

An introduction to the core principles that underpin online teaching for professional adults. This topic covers the basic forces at work that an online mentor should master.

Topic 2 - Using the CMS

This topic deals with the importance of leveraging the Content Management System to interact with students and deliver the content. This explores the central portal that online delivery relies on.

Topic 3 - Slide Decks

A deeper look into how the humble slide deck can be a carefully crafted and powerful tool for delivering content, knowledge and wisdom.

Topic 4 - Live Webinars

The heart of the interaction, the live webinar is an important tool to engage students. This looks at not only how to deliver as a speaker, but how to make best use of the GoToWebinar technology.

Each course is short and to the point. We focus on practical knowledge and useful theory.
